PPE issuance request listing

Online Material Withdrawal System by Philsaga Mining Corporation

  • Click main menu from the sidebar. This part of the OWMS is actually related to Issuance Request Monitoring System (IRMS). Transactions coming from IRMS is being relayed and can be processed inside OWMS. Please see IRMS documentations. BY default view PPE Issuance Request will populate list of records of transactions whether served or unserved.

  • Filter out before doing some searching. By this process you will be able to trim down the viewing into fewer data instead of going over the whole data of details which may not be relevant to you by selecting data from drop-down list.

  • To further trim down the list you may also want to type a keyword on text box what you wanted to search along with the drop-down list data depending on the filtered option made and click .

  • Once you are settled on the searching made you may click any data on rows to be able to see more details of the issuance request.

  • There are records that are not having any status on action colum. It indicates that the transaction is already posted and can no longer be subject for changed and is deemed final. However, those data having a status on action column are still subject for processing and approval. Thus, clicking will redirect you to a new interface.

  • Fill out all relevant information by supplying details to text boxes provided, suppliyng dates, selecting items, code and quanity.
  • You may put check mark on the checkbox if the item is a deduction type of item. Material Control Department (MCD) can decide onto this.
  • You may also opt to release an item and go with the transaction with accounting an item to a department or a person by clicking but this will be subject for approval and justifications to the top management.
  • Once all settled with the details needed click .
